How are transactions executed on Cypher?

WH Cypher was created to offer an alternative way of executing multi-signature transactions. Our mission has begun back when we launched whalesheaven.com, Whalesheaven was dependant on executing deals using 3rd party multi-signature wallets, the only problem was that none of the multisig wallets on the market offered a multi-chain solution for our users which has limited us from listing more coins and tokens across different blockchains.

Today we celebrate the release of the first-ever multi-signature crypto wallet that is designed to operate across any blockchain using threshold signatures, while we keep things simple yet innovative, we are the first to avoid using smart contracts for multi-signature transactions, following our mission of creating a unified way for using multi-signature transactions across all blockchains, while offering a cheaper alternative to costly smart contract transactions.
